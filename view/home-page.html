3. It is good against stress

Perhaps you and your stressed self are not the first to think about reading a sturdy robe when it comes to relaxation, but research at the University of Sussex proves that it is one of the best things to do. For the study, participants underwent various ways to de-stress. After 6 minutes of reading, the stress hormone was 68 percent lower, compared to 61 percent due to listening to music and 42 percent due to a detour. According to the researchers, this is because you briefly escape reality by reading. Which book you read does not matter.
